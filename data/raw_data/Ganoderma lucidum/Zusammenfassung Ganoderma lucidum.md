**Ganoderma curtisii**
*Polyporus curtisii* Berk. (1849)  
*Fomes curtisii* (Berk.) [Cooke](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mordecai_Cubitt_Cooke) (1885)  
*Scindalma curtisii* (Berk.) [Kuntze](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otto_Kuntze) (1898)
Red reishi
Ganoderma fruit bodies and mycelium contain polysaccharides that inhibit tumor growth. Identified as glucans, one of the main constituents of the fungal cell wall, they appear to increase physical resistance to tumor growth, induce interferon production, improve immune function, and destroy tumor cells in the body.
The role of medicinal mushrooms in metabolic regulation is gaining increasing attention. Its use in the development and application of beneficial biological activities has the advantage that the active ingredient is safe and acceptable to humans.

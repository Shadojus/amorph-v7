Golden reishi, yellow reishi
Ganoderma curtisii posseses the same medicinal properties as Ganoderma lucidum.
Lingzhi (Ganoderma lucidum) is a wood mushroom highly prized in traditional medicine and is widely consumed because it is believed to promote health and longevity, reduce the risk of cancer and heart disease, and strengthen the immune system.
It produces polysaccharides and oxygenated triterpenoids with a very broad spectrum of biological activities and pharmacological functions.

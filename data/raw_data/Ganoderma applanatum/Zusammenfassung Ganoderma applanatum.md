Substrates: *Ganoderma applanatum* is a [wood-decay fungus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wood-decay_fungus), causing a rot of [heartwood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heartwood) in a variety of trees. It can also grow as a [pathogen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pathogen) of live [sapwood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sapwood_\(wood\)), particularly on older trees that are sufficiently wet. It is a common cause of decay and death of [beech](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beech) and [poplar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Populus), and less often of several other tree genera, including [alder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alder), [apple](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malus), [elm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elm), [buckeye and horse chestnut](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aesculus), [maple](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maple), [oak](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oak), [walnut](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Walnut), [willow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Willow), [western hemlock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_hemlock), [Douglas fir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Douglas_fir), old or sick [olive tree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olive_tree), and [spruce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spruce).

Medicinical Properties: **Zurzeit werden Lackporlinge wissenschaftlich untersucht, einige Arten sollen antibiotische Wirkung gezeigt haben.**
*Ganoderma applanatum* is known in Japan as *kofuki-saru-no-koshikake* (コフキサルノコシカケ),[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-4)[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-5) literally meaning "powder-covered monkey's bench", and in China as *shu-she-ling-zhi* (树舌灵芝), where it has long been used in traditional medicines.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-http://asiancancerherb.info/-6) Studies have shown *Ganoderma applanatum* contains compounds with potent anti-tumor,[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-7)[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-Antitumor_Activity_of_Ganoderma_applanatum-8)[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-9) antibacterial[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-Antibacterial_Activity-10)[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-antibacterial_properties-11) anti-[fibrotic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fibrosis)[\[12\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ganoderma_applanatum#cite_note-antifibrotic-12) properties

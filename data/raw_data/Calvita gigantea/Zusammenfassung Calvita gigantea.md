Medicinical Properties:
Puffballs are a known [styptic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antihemorrhagic) and have long been used as wound dressing,[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calvatia_gigantea#cite_note-6) either in powdered form or as slices 3 cm thick. The fungus was often harvested prior to battles for this purpose.[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calvatia_gigantea#cite_note-7)
It is the main source of the anti-tumor [mucoprotein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mucoprotein) [calvacin](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Calvacin&action=edit&redlink=1), which is present only in tiny quantities.
